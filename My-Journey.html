My Journey

My journey started with a deep-rooted passion for both the academic and creative worlds, setting me on a path that has been both enriching and extraordinary. At the age of 19, I had already graduated from university with a GPA that not only reflected my hard work but also my ability to excel in challenging environments. This achievement was not just a milestone; it was a launching pad that propelled me into the realms of education and creativity. 

By the age of 20, I had already secured my Philippine Professional Teaching License, a feat that underscored my commitment to my calling as an educator. The journey didn't stop there; by 23, I had added the UAE Certified Teaching License to my achievements, conquering this challenge on my first attempt. These accomplishments were not just personal victories; they were indicators of my ability to adapt, learn, and excel in diverse educational landscapes.

The leap from academia to the professional world was marked by a significant milestone when I became one of the youngest educators hired by the UAE's Ministry of Education, now known as the Emirates Schools Establishment. This transition was not merely a change in title but a testament to my dedication and potential in shaping the future of education.

Throughout this journey, my passion for the creative arts has been a constant companion. Singing, dancing, filmmaking, and photography are not just hobbies; they are extensions of my educational philosophy, blending creativity with learning to inspire and engage students in new and exciting ways.

As I reflect on my journey, from academic achievements to creative endeavors and professional milestones, I am reminded of the power of determination, the importance of passion, and the impact of education. My path has been shaped by a belief in the transformative power of learning and creativity, driving me to continue exploring, growing, and contributing to the fields of education and the arts.

Looking forward, I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead. With each student I teach, each project I undertake, and each challenge I face, I am committed to making a difference, inspired by the journey thus far and eager for the adventures that await. This journey, marked by achievements, creativity, and the pursuit of excellence, is far from over; it is a continuing story of growth, inspiration, and impact.
